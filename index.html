  • Hey there, away from my software engineer idenity— I've got another life as an art enthusiast and painter extraordinaire! 🎨 My journey into the world of art started with a simple curiosity — I couldn't resist capturing the little details that make each face a unique work of living, breathing art.

    But let's be real, my passion didn't stop there. I've embraced the challenge of blending beauty and nature in the most harmonious way possible. That's why I dove headfirst into the world of portraiture and painting. And guess what? I've been rocking those oil paints since 2022 at Diyan Dimitrov's art academy.

    So you will be able to see my progress here on 42 Brush & Whisker.
